Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my ticket? ▾
Log in, go to "My Bookings", select the ticket and click "Cancel". Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Cancellation charges apply per IRCTC policy.
What is the tatkal booking window? ▾
Tatkal quota opens at 10:00 AM one day before the journey for AC classes and 11:00 AM for non-AC classes. An additional tatkal charge is applicable.
Can I modify my travel date after booking? ▾
Modification is currently not available. You will need to cancel the existing ticket (charges apply) and rebook for the new date.
How do I download my e-ticket? ▾
After booking, your e-ticket is emailed automatically. You can also download it from "My Bookings" section. A valid photo ID is required at the time of travel.
Is it safe to travel with a Waitlisted (WL) ticket? ▾
If your WL ticket does not confirm before the chart preparation (usually 4 hours before departure), it is auto-cancelled and refunded. You cannot board the train with a WL ticket.
